Presenting and Critiquing Design

I am interested in presenting and critiquing design topics, my major is media and information, and I concentration on filmmaking. Presenting designs is the same thing as how we learn to pitch our ideas in the film to the investors. I believe that a good design needs a great presenting to support just like a good film idea. From the article attached, there are three great bullet points that could be used in presenting and critiquing design: Use a well-designed presentation template; Have one focal point per slide. Practice going through your presentation once or twice. A great presentation wont have too much text on the screen and should focus on the design itself and the passion the speaker has. If there are multiple points really want to mention specially, put one on each slide and introduce them well, that way it wont be a mess on the screen and the audience would also be focusing on the presentation.
